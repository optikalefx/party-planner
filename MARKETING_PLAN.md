# Mystery Invite — Marketing Plan

**mysteryinvite.com**
*Updated March 2026*

---

## The One-Line Pitch

> **"You bought the kit. Mystery Invite runs the party."**

This is the positioning that separates you from every competitor. Sites like Night of Mystery and My Mystery Party *sell* you the game. Whodunnit and StoryPop *sell* you a script. Nobody owns the coordination layer — the RSVP, the character voting, the themed invite page, the SMS reveals, the pre-party group chat. That's your lane. Stay in it.

---

## Market Context

- Murder mystery games are a **$1.8B+ market growing at 12.6% CAGR**
- Kits are everywhere — Amazon, Five Below, Target — the *content* is a commodity
- The *hosting experience* is still broken: people coordinate over group texts, manually assign characters, and send plain Evite links
- No app currently owns this coordination problem

---

## What You've Built (the full feature story)

Before marketing anything, know what you're selling. Mystery Invite now ships with:

- **AI-generated party themes** — paste a prompt or upload a photo and get a fully styled invite page
- **Invite parsing** — upload your kit's invite image or PDF and the app auto-fills all party details
- **RSVP management** — guests RSVP directly on their invite page
- **Ranked-choice character voting** — guests rank their top character picks; the app runs RCV automatically
- **Blind voting** — optional mode so guests can't see each other's choices
- **Character lock & reveal** — host locks assignments; characters appear on every guest's page instantly
- **SMS notifications** — guests get texts when characters are revealed, when the host posts in chat, when they're mentioned, and a pre-party reminder
- **Real-time group chat** — with emoji reactions, built into the invite page
- **Works with any kit** — Five Below, Amazon, wherever. Bring your own mystery.

## Target Audience

**Primary — The Host Friend (your best customer)**
Women 28–45 who host dinner parties and social gatherings. They've bought a murder mystery kit or are planning to. They care about the *experience* being great, not just functional. They'll tell their friends about tools that made them look good as a host.

**Secondary — Office & Team Event Planners**
Corporate murder mystery team-building is a real and growing segment. Event coordinators are actively searching for tools to make this easier.

**Tertiary — Bachelorette & Birthday Party Organizers**
High-intent planners with a specific occasion, willing to spend time to make it special.

## The Built-In Viral Loop

```
Host buys a kit → Creates party in Mystery Invite
→ Sends invite link to 8–20 guests
→ Guests RSVP, vote on characters, chat before the party
→ Characters revealed by SMS the night before
→ Party happens, everyone has a great time
→ 2–3 guests go home wanting to host their own
→ Cycle repeats
```

Every party you power markets the app to new potential hosts. **The guest-to-host conversion rate is your single most important metric.** Target: 1 new host created per 10 guests.

**Amplify the loop:**
- Add a subtle "Powered by Mystery Invite — host your own" link in the guest page footer
- Send guests a post-party SMS a few days later: *"Had fun? Host your own mystery party at MysteryInvite.com"*

## Channel Strategy

### Tier 1 — Start Here

#### TikTok
The highest-ROI channel for this niche. Murder mystery content performs extremely well — people love the costume reveals, the drama, the group chaos. You don't need a big following to go viral; you need the right moment captured.

**Content that works:**
- *"How I coordinated a murder mystery for 14 people without losing my mind"* (screen-record showing the app)
- Character reveal reaction videos — *"She didn't know she was the murderer until the SMS came through"*
- Time-lapse: invites sent → RSVPs rolling in → voting → reveal night → party footage
- Before/after: group text chaos vs. Mystery Invite

**Key insight:** There is no dominant TikTok creator for murder mystery party *hosting*. First-mover advantage is available right now.

**Hashtags:** `#murdermysteryparty` `#murdermysterydinner` `#dinnerpartyideas` `#partyideas` `#hostingideas` `#gamenight`

**Cadence:** 3–4 posts/week. Consistency beats polish on TikTok.

---

#### Pinterest
High-intent party planners live here. Pinterest traffic compounds over time unlike TikTok — a pin from month 1 will still drive traffic in year 2.

**Content to create:**
- *"Murder Mystery Party Planning Checklist"* (infographic — very pinnable)
- Screenshots of AI-generated themed pages (visually striking, shareable)
- *"How to Assign Characters for a Murder Mystery Party"* step-by-step graphic
- Costume inspiration boards linked back to the app
- Seasonal boards: *"Halloween Murder Mystery Party Ideas"*, *"Winter Dinner Party Mystery"*

**Pinterest SEO:** Pin descriptions must include "murder mystery party," "mystery dinner party," "character assignment," and "party invitation ideas."

**Cadence:** 5–10 pins/day using Tailwind scheduler. Mix your own content with repins.

---

#### Reddit
Slower than TikTok but drives highly engaged signups. You have to earn it.

**Subreddits to engage:**
- r/boardgames (4M+ members) — answer "how do I host a murder mystery?" threads
- r/partyplanning — direct audience
- r/MurderMystery — dedicated niche
- r/entertaining — dinner party hosts

**The play:** Spend 4–6 weeks being genuinely helpful. Answer questions. Share tips. Never mention the app. Once you have karma and credibility, post one honest "I built this because I had this exact problem" post. These posts routinely hit subreddit front pages and drive hundreds of signups in a day.

### Tier 2 — Build Into These

#### SEO / Blog Content
Takes 3–6 months to rank but drives free, sustained traffic forever. Write these five posts first:

1. **"How to Host a Murder Mystery Party: The Complete Guide"**
   High volume, high intent, ranks with thorough content

2. **"How to Assign Characters for a Murder Mystery Party"**
   Low competition, directly solves the problem Mystery Invite addresses

3. **"The Best Murder Mystery Kits on Amazon (Ranked and Reviewed)"**
   Ranks on product searches — everyone who reads it is a perfect Mystery Invite user

4. **"Murder Mystery Party Food and Menu Ideas by Theme"**
   High volume, shareable, easy Pinterest crossover

5. **"Murder Mystery Party Invitation Wording: Templates and Ideas"**
   Direct search intent for what the app does

**Quick technical wins:**
- Include "murder mystery party" in every page title tag
- Launch a blog at mysteryinvite.com/blog
- Add Google Search Console from day one so you can track what's working

---

#### Facebook Groups
Less sexy than TikTok but very high conversion. People in groups are actively planning and trust peer recommendations.

**Groups to join:**
- Search "murder mystery party," "dinner party planning," "party planning [your city]"
- BoardGameGeek community groups
- Bachelorette party planning groups (strong murder mystery crossover)
- Office event planning groups

**Same rule as Reddit:** Give value for weeks before mentioning the app.

---

#### Product Hunt
A strong launch can generate 500–2,000 signups in a day from early adopters who write and share about products they love.

**How to land it:**
- Build supporters before launch day
- Launch Tuesday–Thursday
- Tagline: *"Run your murder mystery party like a pro — invites, characters & SMS reveals in one place"*
- Respond to every comment on launch day
- Have 10+ friends ready to upvote and engage at launch

---

### Tier 3 — Longer Term

#### Micro-Influencer Partnerships
No major influencer owns the murder mystery party hosting niche. This is an open lane.

Target creators with 10k–100k followers in: party planning, dinner party hosting, home entertaining, and "adulting" lifestyle content. Offer free use of the app in exchange for one authentic post showing the guest experience. The AI-generated themed pages are your best visual hook.

#### Seasonal Campaigns
Plan content 4–6 weeks ahead of these peaks:
- **October** — Halloween murder mystery crossover. Biggest spike of the year.
- **November/December** — Holiday dinner party season.
- **February** — Valentine's Day couples mystery dinner.
- **June–August** — Summer backyard entertaining.

## Launch Checklist

### Before You Promote Anything
- [ ] Add "Powered by Mystery Invite" + link to all guest party pages
- [ ] Confirm the invite code entry field is on the landing page
- [ ] Verify SMS delivery is working end-to-end (Twilio A2P registration)

- [ ] Set up Pinterest business account, create first 3 boards, pin daily
- [ ] Join 10 Reddit communities and start engaging (no promotion yet)

### Month 2
- [ ] Publish 2 more blog posts
- [ ] Make your first honest Reddit "I built this" post

- [ ] Plan Product Hunt launch

### Month 3+
- [ ] Product Hunt launch
- [ ] October Halloween content blitz (if timing aligns)

- [ ] Scale what's working, cut what isn't
